How to crash into the wall

Downforce

  • Set to a higher overall setting on twisty tracks, while keeping it lower on downforce tracks

Ride Height

  • Go as low as you can without having the floor scraping on the ground
  • Increase packers/bump stops if bottoming out on bumps
  • Increase heave dampers if bottoming out on bumps.

Tire Pressure

  • Reduce pressure if middle of tire is hotter
  • Increase pressure if middle of tire is colder

Camber

  • Decrease negative camber if inside of tire is hotter by 5Â°C than the outside edge
  • Increase negative camber if outside of tire is hotter than the inside edge

Brakes

  • Reduce brake pressure if you find yourself locking your brakes.
  • Move the brake balance back if you're locking up only the front wheels.
  • Move the brake balance forward if you're locking up rear wheels.

All Speeds

  • Decrease front ride height, take care not to start bottoming out
  • Increase rear ride height
  • Soften front spring compression rate
  • Move brake balance towards the rear
  • Decrease front anti-roll bar stiffness
  • Increase front tire toe-out (higher negative value)
  • Decrease front bump dampers
  • Increase differential coast locking
  • Increase differential pre-load

Mid-High speed

  • Increase rear tire toe-in (higher positive value)
  • Decrease rear downforce
  • Increase front downforce
